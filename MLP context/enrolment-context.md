# Enrolment — Context

---

## 1. Objective

Enable onboarding of employees into insurance coverage.

---

## 2. Core Responsibilities

* Add employees
* Track enrollment completion
* manage enrollment cycles

---

## 3. Key Features (MLP)

### A. Employee Upload

* Bulk upload via Excel
* Template download

---

### B. Enrollment Tracking

* Track:

  * completed
  * pending
* View enrollment status

---

### C. Notifications

* Reminders for incomplete enrollment

---

## 4. Key Problems This Solves

* Manual onboarding
* low enrollment visibility
* HR chasing employees

---

## 5. Dependencies

* HRMS → employee data
* Policy → coverage eligibility
* Endorsements → post-enrollment changes

---

## 6. UX Principles

* Guided workflows
* minimal friction
* clear status visibility

---

## 7. Limitations (Important)

* Not a daily-use module
* Mostly used:

  * during onboarding
  * renewal cycles

---
